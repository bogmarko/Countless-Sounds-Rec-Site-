About us

Countless sounds records is the record label from Belgrade, capital city of Serbia, that deals with publishing and promoting unusual and experimental dance music of various musical genres such as:
  • Electronic
  • House
  • Trance
  • Dubstep
  • Drum and bass
  • Chill
  • Breaks
If you think that you going beyond the boundaries of music you can send us your demo recordings via:
countless.sound@gmail.com
